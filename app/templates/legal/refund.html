Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2024

1. Subscription Model

Invoice Generator SaaS operates on a subscription-based model with the following plans:

  • Free Plan: No charges, no refunds applicable
  • Pro Plan: ₹99 per month, billed monthly

2. Cancellation Policy

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time by:

  • Logging into your account dashboard
  • Going to "Subscription" settings
  • Clicking "Cancel Subscription"
  • Confirming the cancellation

Effect of Cancellation

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
  • You retain access to Pro features until the period expires
  • After expiration, your account reverts to the Free plan
  • Your data and invoices are preserved

3. Refund Policy

General Policy

All subscription fees are generally non-refundable. However, we may provide refunds in the following exceptional circumstances:

Eligible Refund Scenarios

  • Technical Issues: If our service is unavailable for more than 48 consecutive hours due to technical problems on our end
  • Billing Errors: If you were charged incorrectly due to a system error
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were charged multiple times for the same subscription period
  • Unauthorized Charges: If charges were made without your authorization (subject to investigation)

Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be provided for:

  • Change of mind or dissatisfaction with features
  • Failure to use the service during the billing period
  • Violation of Terms of Service leading to account suspension
  • Partial usage of the billing period
  • Technical issues on the user's end (internet, device, browser)

4. Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact our support team at support@invoicegen.com
  2. Provide your account email and transaction details
  3. Explain the reason for the refund request
  4. Provide any supporting documentation

Processing Timeline

  • Review: We will review your request within 5 business days
  • Decision: You will be notified of our decision via email
  • Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Credit: Refunds appear in your account within 5-7 business days after processing

5. Prorated Refunds

In exceptional cases where a refund is approved:

  • Refunds may be prorated based on unused service time
  • The calculation will be based on the remaining days in the billing cycle
  • Minimum refund amount is ₹10 (processing costs)

6. Payment Method Refunds

Credit/Debit Cards

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • Timeline depends on your bank's processing time
  • May take 5-10 business days to reflect

UPI/Net Banking

  • Refunds processed through the original payment method
  • Usually faster than card refunds (2-5 business days)

Wallets

  • Refunded to the original wallet
  • Usually instant or within 24 hours

7. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute:

  • Please contact us first to resolve the issue
  • Chargebacks may result in account suspension
  • We will provide transaction documentation to your bank
  • Resolution may take 30-90 days depending on your bank

8. Free Trial and Promotional Offers

  • Free trials can be cancelled anytime without charges
  • Promotional pricing is non-refundable
  • Special offers cannot be combined with refund requests

9. Service Credits

Instead of monetary refunds, we may offer:

  • Service credits for future billing periods
  • Extended subscription periods
  • Upgraded features at no additional cost

10. Force Majeure

We are not liable for refunds due to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government regulations
  • Internet service provider issues
  • Third-party service failures beyond our control

11. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions:

  • Email: support@invoicegen.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted on our website
  • Emailed to active subscribers
  • Effective 30 days after notification

We strive to provide excellent service and will work with you to resolve any legitimate concerns about billing or service quality.
