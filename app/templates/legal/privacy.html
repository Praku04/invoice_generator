Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Company information (name, address, GSTIN, etc.)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Razorpay)
  • Profile pictures, logos, and stamps you upload

Invoice Data

We store the invoices you create, including:

  • Client information
  • Invoice items and amounts
  • Tax calculations
  • Generated PDF files

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Login times and session duration
  • Error logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Generate and store your invoices
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve our Service and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Razorpay for payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers for data storage
  • Email service providers for notifications

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure file upload and storage

5. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Account data: Until you delete your account
  • Invoice data: For 7 years for tax compliance
  • Payment records: As required by financial regulations
  • Usage logs: For 2 years for security purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your invoice data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Restrict processing of your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services

Razorpay

Payment processing is handled by Razorpay. Their privacy policy governs how they handle payment information.

External Links

Our Service may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored in India. If transferred internationally, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Adequacy decisions
  • Appropriate safeguards

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification
  • In-app notification
  • Website banner

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@invoicegen.com
  • Address: Mumbai, India
  • Response time: Within 30 days

13. Data Protection Officer

For data protection matters, contact our DPO at dpo@invoicegen.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Invoice Generator SaaS collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.
